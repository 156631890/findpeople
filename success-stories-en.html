A 30-Year Journey: A Family's Reunion Story

In 1990, Mrs. Li was separated from her 5-year-old son due to an accident. For 30 years, she never gave up searching, but with limited information technology at the time, there was no progress. In early 2024, with one last hope, Mrs. Li contacted FindPeople.

Our professional team analyzed historical archives, social networks, and DNA databases, eventually finding her son in Henan Province. After DNA confirmation, the mother-son relationship was verified.

"For 30 years, I thought about him every day, dreaming of seeing him again. Thanks to FindPeople, I finally got to experience this day." — Mrs. Li

This reunion not only changed the lives of Mrs. Li and her son but also made us deeply appreciate the meaning of our work. No matter how long it takes, we will do our utmost to help every family reunite.
